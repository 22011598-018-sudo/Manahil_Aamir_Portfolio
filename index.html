About Me

01 / SYS

I'm Manahil Aamir, a final-year BS Software Engineering student at the University of Gujrat, Pakistan, and a full-stack developer with end-to-end product ownership experience — from normalised database schema design to polished mobile UI. I'm currently working as a Front-End AI Intern at FlyRank AI (Chicago, USA, remote), building AI-powered React UI for a US SaaS product, alongside a Cybersecurity Analyst Intern role at DevelopersHub Corporation and a Cybersecurity Intern role at Softbond Digital.

Beyond code, I organize things — I led DineEase from prototype to the international stage at ICSDI Conference, Prince Sultan University (Riyadh), hosted a university event with the AIG New York CEO Council, ran our Final Year Project Exhibition, and moderated a Software Reliability Engineering competition. I like being in the room where things get built, whether that's a codebase or an event.
